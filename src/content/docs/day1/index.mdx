<Aside type="tip" title="The Big Question">
Your application works on your laptop. Your teammate pulls the same code, runs it, and it breaks. A missing library, a different OS version, a conflicting port. **Why does this keep happening?**
</Aside>

## What You Will Learn Today

Day 1 answers a fundamental question: **how do we make software run the same way everywhere?**

By the end of today, you will understand:

- **Why** containers were invented and what problem they solve
- **How** containers differ from virtual machines — and why containers won
- **How** Docker is architected — the client, daemon, and registry model
- **What** images and containers are — blueprints vs running instances
- **How** containers move through their lifecycle — creation to removal

## The Story So Far

You have a microservice application:

- A **React frontend** that serves the UI
- A **Node.js API** that handles business logic
- A **Redis cache** for fast lookups
- A **PostgreSQL database** for persistent storage

It works on your machine. Today, we start the journey of making it work **on any machine**.
